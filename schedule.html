SCHEDULE

Here is where you'll find information about any of our ongoing events. Check back here often and like our Facebook Page to stay in the know.

First Saturday Open House

WHERE: Rochester Vegan Community Center
WHEN: 11PM - 5PM every 1st Saturday of the month

Visit to shop local businesses, talk to nonprofit organizations, discover new favorite products, and of course eat some amazing vegan food.

Second-Saturday Vegan Bring Your Own | Hosted by Animal Rights Rochester

WHERE: Rochester Vegan Community Center
WHEN: 1PM, every 2nd Saturday of the month

This is a pot-luck style event so please bring a vegan dish or two to share. We reccomend including a recipe so people can be mindful of allergens, oil, or gluten if they're so inclined. Plus everyone gets to learn how to make some delicious vegan food! Suggested donation of $5.
