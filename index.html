We're also excited to announce that coming April 14th to Rochester is a wonderful project by our friends at Open the Cages Alliance: the completely free Vegan Living Program!

VLP is a community-building vegan mentorship program. It features a month of in-person educational events that cover the hows and whys of vegan living, including the ethical and environmental impact of veganism, nutrition, cooking, meal planning, and being vegan in a not-yet-vegan world.

But there's no reason to wait; you can also take part in the 21 Days of Love, 3 week Vegan Challenge on Facebook. Once you join the page you'll be placed in a group of other new vegans, with a mentor where you will discuss topics about veganism, share recipes, ask questions, and gain important knowledge during your first 21 days being vegan.
